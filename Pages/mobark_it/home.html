Information Technology (IT)

        is transforming our world, changing the way we do business, the way we learn, the way we communicate, and even the way we entertain ourselves.The IT discipline addresses the integration, design, deployment, and management of computing and networking resources and services, as well as the development of technology infrastructures in organizations.

       The field of IT has applications ranging across the entire economic spectrum. IT specialists solve complex software and hardware problems that require fundamental knowledge and competencies and utilize the processes of needs assessment, technology transfer, and user support.
